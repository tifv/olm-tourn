\problem
Let $P$ be an arbitrary point inside a tetrahedron $ABCD$.
Denote by $R$ the circumradius of the tetrahedron and by $x$ the distance from
$P$ to the circumcenter.
Prove the inequalities
\[
   (R + x) \cdot (R - x)^3
\leq
   PA \cdot PB \cdot PC \cdot PD
\leq
   (R + x)^3 \cdot (R - x)
.\]
\solution
\endproblem
