\problem\problemscore{8}
Three runners are training on a straight track.
They have different (constant) speeds.
When any of them reaches an end of the track, he immediately turns around and runs back, then turns around at the other end of the track, and so on.  It occured that five times all the runners found themselves at one point.
Prove that there will be infinite number of such meetings.
